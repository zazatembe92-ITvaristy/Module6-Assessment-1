ANIMALS MAP PLACES

LION

Is the largest cat of the genus Panthera native to Africa and India.It has a muscular,deep-chested body,short,rounded head,round ears and hairy tuft at the end of its tail.It is sexually dimorphic;adult male lions are larger than females and have a prominent mane.It is a social species,forming groups called prides.A lion's pride consists of a few adult males,related females and cubs.Groups of female lions usually hunt together,preying mostly on large ungulates.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

Lions live in a variety habitats but prefer grasslands,savanna,dense scrub and open woodland.
