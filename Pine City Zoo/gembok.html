GEMSBOK

The gemsbok is a large antelope in the genus Oryx. It is native to the arid regions of Southern Africa,such as the Kalahari Desert.Some authorities formerly included the East African oryx as a subspecies.The gemsbok is depicted on the coat of arms of Namibia,where the current population of the species is estimated at 373,000 individuals.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find gemsbok on the grasslands or savannas and on flat or hilly country in the Kalahari Desert in Southern Africa.
