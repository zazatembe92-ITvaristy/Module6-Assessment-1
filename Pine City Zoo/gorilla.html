ANIMALS MAP PLACES

GORILLA

Gorillas are the largest living primates,reaching heights between 1.25-1.8 meters,weights between 100-270 kg,and arm span up to 2.6 meters,depending on species and sex.The Eastern gorilla is distinguished from the Western by darker fur colour and some other minor morphological differences.Gorillas tends to live 35-40 years in the wild.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find gorillas in dense forests and lowland swamps and marshes as low as sea level,with Western lowland gorillas living in Central West African contries and Eastern lowland gorillas living in the Democratic Republic of the Congo near its border with Rwanda.
