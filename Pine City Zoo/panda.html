PANDA

Panda also known as panda bear.It is characterised by its bold black-and-white coat and rotund body.The name "giant panda" is sometimes used to distinguish it from the red panda,a neighbouring musteloid.Though it belongs to the order Carnivora,the giant panda is a folivore,with bamboo shoots and leaves making up more than 99% of its diet.[7] giant pandas in the wild will occasionally eat other grasses.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

You will find pandas in temperature forests high in mountains of Southwest China.
